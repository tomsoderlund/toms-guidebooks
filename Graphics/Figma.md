### Elements

Structure:

    ᠅ Element1 (component)
        ⬚ Normal (group)
            ▭ Normal (e.g. text, rectangle – NOT a group)
            ▭ Normal (e.g. text, rectangle – NOT a group)
            ▭ :hover (e.g. text, rectangle – NOT a group)
            ▭ _NotExported
            ▭ _:hover (not exported)
        ⬚ OtherVariant (group)
    ᠅ Element2 (component)
        ⬚ Normal (group)
        ⬚ OtherVariant (group)
